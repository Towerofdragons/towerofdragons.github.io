Enchipai Ahadi Mara

Enchipai Ahadi Mara is a serene and luxurious eco-lodge nestled in the Oloololo Escarpment right in the heart of the Maasai Mara. Offering breathtaking views of the savannah, this resort provides a perfect blend of comfort and nature. Enchipai Ahadi Mara is renowned for its commitment to sustainable tourism and its authentic African experiences, from guided safari tours to cultural immersions with the local Maasai community. Whether you're looking for adventure or relaxation, this retreat promises an unforgettable stay amidst the natural wonders of Kenya.

Here's some of the Key Features of Enchipai Ahadi Mara

Luxury Tented Accommodation

Elegantly furnished tents with en-suite bathrooms and private verandas overlooking the Maasai Mara.

Eco-Friendly Design

Committed to sustainability, the lodge utilizes solar power and eco-friendly practices throughout its operations.

Authentic Safari Experiences

Guided safari tours to explore the Big Five and the annual wildebeest migration.

Cultural Immersion

Engage with the Maasai community through traditional dances, village visits, and cultural workshops.

Fine Dining

Enjoy a blend of international and African cuisine with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Exclusive Bush Experiences

Bush breakfasts, sundowners, and private dinners for a fully immersive experience.

Outdoor Pool

A relaxing pool with views of the Mara plains, perfect for spotting wildlife while you unwind.
