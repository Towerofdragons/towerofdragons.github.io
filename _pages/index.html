About

The Maze isn’t about solving mazes—it’s about exploring the concept of movement, space, and choice within a constrained environment. Built in C using SDL2, the game serves as a minimalist experiment in navigation and interaction, focusing on how simple mechanics and sparse design can lead to a deeper experience of spatial awareness and decision-making.

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School through ALX Africa software engineering
